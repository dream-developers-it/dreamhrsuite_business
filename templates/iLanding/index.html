How does the employee monitoring system work?

Our system tracks employee activities through secure software that monitors screen activity, application usage, and productivity metrics. All data is encrypted and stored securely, with customizable privacy settings to protect employee information.

What features are included in the time tracking system?

Our time tracking system includes automated attendance recording, break time monitoring, overtime calculation, leave management, and detailed activity reports. It integrates seamlessly with payroll processing for accurate compensation.

How secure is the employee data in your system?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data centers, regular backups, and role-based access control. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations to ensure your employee data remains safe.

Can managers generate custom reports for employee performance?

Yes, our system offers comprehensive reporting tools where managers can create customized reports based on various metrics including productivity, attendance, project progress, and performance indicators. Reports can be scheduled or generated on-demand.

Is the system suitable for remote team management?

Absolutely! Our system is specifically designed to support remote work environments with features like remote activity monitoring, virtual time tracking, project management tools, and communication features to keep remote teams connected and productive.

What kind of customer support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including live chat, email, and phone. Our dedicated support team provides quick resolution to technical issues and helps with system setup, training, and ongoing assistance.
